About Sonar

This visualization demonstrates a sonar radar sweep, which is the inspiration for the Sonar Visit algorithm - a constant-time approach to the Traveling Salesman Problem.

Key concepts:

  • The rotating beam performs a 360-degree sweep
  • Objects (ships) are detected at their angular position
  • Each point has coordinates (x, y) and a pre-calculated angle from center
  • The sweep visits points in angular order, connecting them to form a tour
